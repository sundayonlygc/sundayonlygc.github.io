Important News

A piece of good news; Edgar has volunteered to step up and take on the role of Treasurer. Thanks much, Edgar!

Joe Morse had earlier offered to take on the role of scheduling our tournaments so it does not appear that this will be the club's last year.

Upcoming Tournaments

September's Tournament will be held at Poppy Hills on Sunday, September 15th at 10:30 AM.

It is imperative that you arrive no later than 30 minutes prior to the first tee time. Failure to do so causes significant inconvenience for those responsible for collecting money and checking people in, as they are unable to warm up. Additionally, arriving early will not only alleviate this issue but also enable you to play better.
