Highlights

Birdie or Better Shooters: Out of 19 players, 11 achieved birdies or better.

3 Putts: Ted Escobar was the only player to avoid a 3-putt all day—impressive!

Chip Ins: It seems the holes on the green were more forgiving this time, as four players managed to chip in! Excellent precision from Dave Gimmick, Rafael Torres, and Craig Citron. And a special shoutout to Ray Gilcrest, who managed to card two zero-putts—remarkable!

How Did We Do? Hole 10 proved to be the toughest challenge of the day, with players averaging 2 strokes over par. The putting struggles were real, with the club averaging nearly 3 putts per player on that hole—a statistic boosted by Antonio Villa and Craig Cytron, who each carded 6 putts. The next hardest hole was Hole 7, averaging 1.5 strokes over par.

In contrast, Hole 9 was the easiest, likely aided by a favorable tailwind for those long drives, with an average of just 0.4 strokes over par.

Low Gross: Congratulations to Edgar Alaniz for claiming the low gross title, edging out Senan King on the scorecard! Both players posted a gross score of 73, but Edgar’s steady play on the back nine, finishing even par, gave him the edge over Senan, who shot 4 over.
