Upcoming Tournaments

It is imperative that you arrive no later than 30 minutes prior to the first tee time. Failure to do so causes significant inconvenience for those responsible for collecting money and checking people in, as they are unable to warm up. Additionally, arriving early will not only alleviate this issue but also enable you to play better.

Match Play 2022/2023

Final 2023 Match Play Results: Congratulations to Dave Dorr, who emerged as the champion of our 2023 Match Play competition! Dave's outstanding performance in this year's matches led him to victory, marking his first Match Play Championship since 1999. Well done, Dave!

Tournament Disruptions: Unfortunately, we faced some disruptions this year due to bad weather, causing us to miss two tournaments. Additionally, we had to disqualify 11 players for missing at least two tournaments in a row. While these decisions are never easy, they are in line with our club's rules and regulations.

Looking Ahead to 2024: As we close the chapter on the 2023 season, we're already gearing up for an exciting 2024 campaign. Our plans include hosting matches in December, right before our November tournament. Stay tuned for more details and updates as we prepare for another year of thrilling golfing experiences.

How Did We Do? We observed that holes 6 and 8 proved to be the easiest for our golfers, averaging just 0.2 strokes over par. On the flip side, holes 4 and 18 presented a more formidable challenge, with an average of 1.2 strokes over par.

Low Gross: In a stunning display of skill and precision, Ryan Torres has claimed the title of low gross with an impressive score of 69, an outstanding 4 strokes under par. His remarkable performance is a testament to his golfing prowess, and we congratulate him on this exceptional achievement..

Team Game - 2 Person Better Ball

In an exciting turn of events, we had a thrilling tie at 64 between two exceptional pairs: Dave Dorr & Dave Dimmick and Joe Morris & Jesse Alaniz. However, when it came to the tiebreaker, the back 9 scores proved decisive, with Joe & Jesse securing victory with a remarkable score of 32, compared to 35 for Dave & Dave. Congratulations to Joe Morris and Jesse Alaniz!

Skins

In the spirited battle for skins, here are the results:

- Dave Dorr claimed a total of 4 skins on holes 2, 5, 9, and 12.

- Jesse Alaniz secured 2 skins on holes 1 and 7.

- Mark Reyes earned a skin with a strong performance on hole 8.

- John Roberts added to the tally with a skin on hole 6.

- John Wei rounded off the skins game with a victory on hole 16.

A grand total of 9 skins were won, each valued at $7. Congratulations to all the winners for their exceptional performances on the course!
