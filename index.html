Important News

Good news: Edgar has volunteered to step up and take on the role of Treasurer. Thank you, Edgar! Joe Morse has also offered to handle tournament scheduling, ensuring that this won't be the club's last year.

Upcoming Tournaments

September's Tournament will be held at Poppy Hills on Sunday, September 15th, at 10:00 AM.

It is imperative that you arrive no later than 30 minutes before the first tee time. Arriving late causes significant inconvenience for those responsible for collecting money and checking people in, as they are unable to warm up. Arriving early will not only alleviate this issue but also enable you to play better.
